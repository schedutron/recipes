Ingredients (for 2 cups of tea):
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon tea leaves
- pinch of finely chopped ginger (optional)

Directions:
1. Put 1 cup water in a reasonably-sized boiling utensil and put the utensil on stove. Switch on the stove.
2. After you see some agitation in water, add 2 teaspoons of sugar in it.
3. Subsequently, add 1 teaspoon of tea leaves.
4. Wait for little more agitation.
5. Add 1 cup milk. Agitation should stop.
6. Wait for the mixture to boil.
7. Once it starts boiling, you may add your pinch of ginger (medically useful). Wait a minute or two.
8. Switch the stove off, and carefully serve the tea in cups. Then enjoy the drink!
